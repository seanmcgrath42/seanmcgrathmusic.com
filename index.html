Sean McGrath Music

Original folk songs, written in Ireland, shared freely with the world.

This is a catalogue of original songs — ninety of them across nine batches — written and recorded by Sean McGrath. None of this is for sale. All of it is CC BY: record it, arrange it, perform it, share it. Just say where it came from.

Each song has its story, a YouTube recording, and everything a musician needs to make their own version — lyrics, chords, ChordPro file, and MP3.
