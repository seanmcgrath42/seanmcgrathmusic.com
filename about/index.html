The Songs

Ninety original songs, organised into nine batches of ten. They're all here — lyrics, chords, recordings, and the stories behind them.

Free to Use

Every song on this site is released under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (CC BY) licence. That means you can record it, perform it, arrange it, share it, or build on it — for any purpose, including commercially — as long as you credit Sean McGrath as the original writer.

If you make a cover or recording, get in touch — it would be great to feature it here.

Downloads

Each song page has the chord chart displayed inline, a ChordPro file for download, and an MP3 of the original recording. A complete songbook PDF of all 90 songs is also available.
